Age Suitability

Time Capsule: Memory Vault

Time Capsule is suitable for all ages. This page provides the information you may need for the App Store's Age Suitability (optional) field.

Age Rating

Time Capsule is designed for users of all ages. The app contains no mature, violent, or inappropriate content. It is commonly used by families, schools, and individuals of any age.

Content

All content in the app is created by the user and stored privately on their device. There is no user-generated content visible to others, no social features, no chat, and no advertising. Users add their own photos, videos, voice memos, and notes to personal time capsules that only they can open.

Summary

  • No mature or objectionable content
  • No social sharing or public content
  • No advertising
  • 100% on-device and private
