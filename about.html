Why Time Capsule?

We take hundreds of photos, record countless moments, and write things we quickly forget. Most of these memories get buried in camera rolls, never to be seen again.

Time Capsule changes that. Instead of letting memories fade into an endless scroll, you seal them with intention. You choose a date. You lock them away. And when the time comes, you rediscover them with the excitement of opening a real time capsule.

No social media. No sharing. No cloud. Just you and your memories, preserved exactly as you left them.
