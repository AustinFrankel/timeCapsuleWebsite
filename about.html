Why Time Capsule?

We take hundreds of photos, record countless moments, and write things we quickly forget. Most of these memories get buried in camera rolls, never to be seen again. We scroll past them without stopping. The moments that once meant everything become background noise in an infinite feed.

Time Capsule changes that. Instead of letting memories fade into an endless scroll, you seal them with intention. You pick photos from a birthday dinner, record a voice memo to your future self, write a note about how you're feeling right now. You choose a date — maybe a month from now, maybe ten years. Then you lock it all away.

When the time comes, you rediscover everything inside with the excitement of opening a real time capsule. You'll hear your own voice from years ago. You'll see photos you forgot you took. You'll read words that hit differently with distance.

No social media. No sharing. No cloud. Just you and your memories, preserved exactly as you left them.

Time Capsule was created by Austin Frankel, who wanted to build something personal in a world full of apps designed to keep you scrolling. The goal was simple: make preserving memories feel as special as rediscovering them.

Made for every kind of memory

Whether it's just for you or a group of people, Time Capsule fits into how you already live.

Friend Groups

Seal inside jokes, trip photos, and group memories. Set a date and open them together at your next reunion.

Schools & Classrooms

Students can capture a school year and open it at graduation. Teachers can create capsules for end-of-year surprises.

Personal Journaling

Write letters to your future self. Record how you feel today and rediscover your growth months or years later.

Families

Capture milestones — first steps, birthday parties, holiday traditions — and lock them for future family gatherings.

Workplaces & Teams

Seal team photos, project milestones, or launch day celebrations. Open them at the company anniversary.

Couples & Relationships

Lock away date night photos, love letters, and voice messages. Open them on anniversaries for a meaningful surprise.

What we believe in

Intentional Design

Every screen, animation, and interaction was designed to make the experience feel personal and meaningful — not addictive.

Simplicity

Add your memories, set a date, lock it. That's it. The best apps stay out of your way.
