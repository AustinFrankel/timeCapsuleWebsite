About Time Capsule

Memories deserve to be
rediscovered

Time Capsule is a simple idea: capture what matters today, lock it away, and experience the joy of opening it in the future.

Why Time Capsule?

We take hundreds of photos, record countless moments, and write things we quickly forget. But most of these memories get buried in our camera rolls, never to be seen again.

Time Capsule changes that. Instead of letting memories fade into a scroll, you seal them with intention. You choose a date. You lock them away. And when the time comes, you rediscover them with the excitement of opening a real time capsule.

No social media. No sharing. No cloud. Just you and your memories, preserved exactly as you left them.
